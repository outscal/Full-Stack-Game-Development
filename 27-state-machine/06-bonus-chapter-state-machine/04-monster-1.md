You need to create a new enemy from scratch with even more complex states as described below. 

This new enemy is a monster called **Titanis **because of it's huge size and rampage behavior.

This creature is a massive, mutated monstrosity. Its states are designed to showcase a mix of aggressive, defensive, and strategic behaviors.



![](//outscal.github.io/Full-Stack-Game-Development/images/182605bc97a1ceda.gif)



Create the following additional states for the **Titanis**:

1. **Roaring Intimidation:** Upon player detection, the **Titanis** enters a state of roaring, causing nearby objects to tremble. This state doesn't involve direct attacks but increases the player's fear, making them temporarily slower.
2. **Charge Attack:** The **Titanis** charges at the player with immense speed, smashing through obstacles in its path. It uses the "Melee Attack" animation during this state.
3. **Adaptive Defense:** When low on health, the **Titanis** enters a defensive state where it becomes less hostile and receives lesser damage than usual.
4. **Rampage:** At intervals, the **Titanis** undergoes a rampage, temporarily gaining increased speed and strength. During this state, it alternates between melee attacks and Charge Attacks.
