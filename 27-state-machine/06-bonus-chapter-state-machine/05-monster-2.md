You need to create a new enemy from scratch with even more complex states as described below. 

This new enemy is a monster called **Venomar **because of it's venomous attacks and corrupting influence.

This creature is smaller but highly agile and erratic in its behavior.

![](https://tenor.com/view/gjirlfriend-eddie-brock-venom-gif-24175957.gif)



Create the following additional states for the **Venomar**:

1. **Infectious Frenzy:** When first encountering the player, **Venomar** enters a frenzied state, attacking wildly with melee swipes and throws pieces of its own body as ranged attacks.
2. **Diseased Evasion:** When the player lands multiple consecutive hits, the **Venomar** enters a state of evasive maneuvers, dodging attacks and regaining some health. Will occur only once for each enemy.
3. **Gooey Trap:** The **Venomar** creates a sticky, gooey trap on the ground. If the player steps into it, they get slowed down significantly, allowing the **Venomar** to catch up and execute a melee attack.
4. **Toxic Emission:** In this state, the **Venomar** emits a toxic vomit on player, causing damage over time to the player if they stay within its vicinity.
