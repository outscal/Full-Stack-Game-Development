Get ready to send shivers down players' spines with these new horrors. Here's your task:

**Design five new horror events** that will push the player's sanity to its limits within the expanded horror mansion.



![](https://tenor.com/view/cj-gif-22858252.gif)



**1. Creepy Doll Event:**

- A doll suddenly appears in the player's path, following their movements with its gaze.
- Player's insanity increases moderately.
- Doll's Eyes Glow Red.
- Soft but Ominous Sound Cues.
- Doll moves slightly when the player isn't looking directly at it.



![](https://tenor.com/view/annabelle-the-conjuring-creepy-doll-doll-creepy-gif-6423220.gif)



**2. Haunted Painting Event:**

- As the player approaches a painting, the figures in the painting start moving and reaching out.
- Player's insanity increases moderately.
- Figure in the painting disappears.
- Distorted, Echoing Laughter Sound Cues.
- Furniture Floats or Moves.



![](https://tenor.com/view/girl-painting-scary-boo-halloween-gif-14057389.gif)



**3. Hall of Whispers Event:**

- Upon entering a particular hallway, a cacophony of whispers and eerie sounds surround the player.
- Muffled whispers begin echoing around the player, seemingly coming from all directions.
- Player's insanity increases gradually.
- Flickering Lights.
- Echoing Footsteps.



**4. Clockwork Disturbance Event:**

- Player approaches a giant clock in a room/hallway. Nothing happens. 
- After that, when player isn't directly looking at the clock (turns around), start chiming erratically and the hands spin wildly.
- Player's insanity increases sharply.
- Distorted Ticking Sound Cues.



**5. Haunted Music Box Event:**

- A music box starts playing a haunting melody on its own as the player approaches.
- Player's insanity increases moderately.
- Melody becomes dissonant and eerie.
- Objects in the room start floating or shaking in time with the music.
- Music stops suddenly, plunging the room into silence.
