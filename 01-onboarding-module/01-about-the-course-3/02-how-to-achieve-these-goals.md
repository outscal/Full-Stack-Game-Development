The way to upskill yourself in the art of making games is to simply keep making **more game projects** while **learning new things** and pushing your boundaries each time. 



**During this course you will be: **

- Making game projects in C++ & Unity 
- Learning **C++**, **Unity**, **Data Structures** and **Design Patterns** from scratch.
- Going through more than 20 guided game projects



**Here is a quick breakup of projects grouped by Topics:**

- 3 Projects in C++ Modules
- 3 Projects in Unity Modules
- 8 Projects in Data Structure Modules
- 6 Projects in Design Pattern Modules



Apart from these guided game projects you are also going to make a lot of personal game projects as assignments after each module.



By the end of this course your portfolio will be so big and diverse that you will be able to impress a lot of recruiters in the gaming industry.
