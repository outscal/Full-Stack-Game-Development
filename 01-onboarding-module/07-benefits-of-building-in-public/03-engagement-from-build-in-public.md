Every post is an opportunity to start a conversation and gain valuable feedback.



Discussing specific game mechanics, designs or coding concepts can provide useful input for improving your skills.



**You have a choice:** be an average bystander, or share your journey and become the main character of your own story.



**Know that people want to, and are waiting to see your journey. They'll relate, connect, celebrate, encourage and give feedback that helps you grow.**
