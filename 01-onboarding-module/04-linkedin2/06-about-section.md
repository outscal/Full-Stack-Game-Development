# **About Section**

- This is the first section that will go a little bit further into the details about who you are as an individual.
- Some of the key things that you want to highlight in the about section is a slightly longer version of the tagline formulated as a full sentence.
- Start with that, because this essentially defines who you are.
- You want to explain a little bit about what you do.
- Highlighting outcomes and achievements is super important.
- You can mention which technologies you are excited about and what are you exploring right now.
- In the last line dont forget to leave out your contact information.
- If others are interested in connecting with you, how can they reach out. You can drop your email ID or contact information.
