# **Past Experience**

- The experience section is one of the most important sections in your profile.
- It basically is a window into your past and gives the reader an idea about
- - what kind of things have you worked on in your past
  - what kind of learnings did you get out of these?
- This section is not for only full time jobs.
- Any small internships, part-time-work, freelance work, or even if you worked with any non-profit organiztion, etc. all of these account to your professional experience.
- Make sure you highlight which company you have been working with.
- Majority people end up filling details about the company itself, but what they often miss out is that, this is your experience.
- This is **not** a snapshot of what the company is about this is a snapshot of:
- - what were the responsibilities that you had
  - how you were able to make an impact to the company to the product
  - what kind of outcomes were you able to achieve. Take a look at an example below:
