Have you ever seen a recording of yourself answering interview questions? Probably not. 



But what if you could see that? That would help you  improve a lot! 

You could assess what your mistakes are from a third person's perspective and get a chance to improve upon it. 

Self-Interviews are a mean to do just that. 



In each Self-Interview assignment you will be required to record a video of yourself (Use Loom) answering some technical questions.



You will evaluate yourself and submit a video that you are satisfied with.



The purpose of this exercise is to become aware of your own shortcomings and improve upon those before any external evaluator comes steps in.
