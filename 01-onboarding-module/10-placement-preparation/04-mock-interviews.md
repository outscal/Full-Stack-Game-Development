At the end of each Module you will be having a Technical Mock Interview.



This Mock Interview is going to be treated as an actual professional interview.



You will be asked any questions related to game dev and whatever you have learnt so far.



You will book an interview slot with a working professional in the gaming industry. (The link will be provided to you)



The mock interview will be of approx. 30 minutes and may be extended further if necessary.



According to your answers, you will be given a status of ‘**Passed**’ or ‘**Failed**’ by the interviewer along with some **detailed feedback**.



In case you fail the mock interview, you will need to prepare again according to the feedback given and schedule another interview.



**You won't be allowed to move top the next module if you are not able to clear the Mock Interview.**
