Apart from your profiles you will be also upskilled in various other aspects.



Here are a few of those things that you will encounter during placement modules:

- **Non-Technical QnA:** A lot of people get rejected in Cultural/Non-Technical Interview Rounds even after all the technical rounds. You will get to know what kind of tricky questions are asked in these Non-Technical rounds and how to answer them.
- **HR Mock Rounds:** You will be paired with an actual HR Manager working in the gaming industry for a mock interview. 
  They will test you and assess you during the interview. According to the result you will be allowed to proceed or prepare again and re-apply.
- **Live Coding Tests:** You will be getting on a call with an interviewer and they will ask you to share your screen and solve a coding problem of their choice, live in the interview.
  They will test you and assess you during the interview. According to the result you will be allowed to proceed or prepare again and re-apply.
- **Negotiation Techniques:** You will be trained to handle tricky negotiation techniques to get the best possible salary for your skills.
- **Email Writing:** You will get to know how to send cold emails and how to keep the conversation rolling in a professional manner while communicating with recruiters and companies regarding placements and job opportunities.
