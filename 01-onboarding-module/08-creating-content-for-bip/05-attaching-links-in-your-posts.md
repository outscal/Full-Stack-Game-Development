In this content you will learn how to attach links in your posts to get the most advantage in reach. Adding links in your posts directly can mess up your post’s engagement and reach, here is how to avoid it:

- When you add links of GitHub, Replit, or any other thing in your posts, it will not get that much boost.
- LinkedIn doesn’t like it when its users are getting redirected out of its platform.
- So LinkedIn’s algorithm discourages and does not show your posts to others if you have added external links in your posts.
- There is a way around it though, add all your relevant link in a comment under the comment section as shown in the following example:

- You will get benefit in reach by adding images, carousels, or videos instead of external adding links to all of them.
- So if you want to create a post about your code on GitHub or Replit, Attach a screenshot of your code in the the post and mention to “Check out the link to my game/code in the comment section below.” And give the link as a comment under that post.
