Many people are able to understand the importance of building in public but they are not able to figure out how to start or how should they create content for a post. After going through this chapter this problem will be solved.



The reason for you to post should not only be to impress people about your skills. Do you know what people are most impressed about on social media? Just like while watching an anime, the backstory of the character is the part which most people love, right? **A story with a Human touch**.



In the past, over the years we have seen thousands of posts from our students and one thing we have learnt is that the audience likes to see content that has a human side to it.



For example, You coded some really good stuff? In that case, simply telling people to check your code and adding your GitHub link on the post might or might not work.


But if you add **why** you decided to make this game, what was your thought process behind that decision, what was the motivation and **how do you think you grew as a game developer** while making this game.



Then for sure the audience will be interested to know about your journey and you will build more trust and engagement with your audience. And in turn all this engagement over time will bring you a lot of job offers directly in your DMs.



Because at the end we all want to see you getting placed in your dream gaming studio.



In short, it is about your **Journey** rather than your **Achievements**. Your journey is what people want to see, and become a part of.
