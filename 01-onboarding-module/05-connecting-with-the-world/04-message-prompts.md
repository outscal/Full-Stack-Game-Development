# **Message Prompts**



If you are finding it difficult to break the ice, Don't worry, Here are some message prompts that you can use while connecting with people for the first time:

- "Hello [Their Name], I am an inspired game developer and would love to connect with talented and like minded people in the industry.”
- "Hello [Their Name], I found your recent post very informative. It would be great to be in your network to continue learning from you.”
- "Hello [Their Name], I noticed your expertise in game programming. Being an aspiring game developer myself, I'd love to connect and learn from you."
- "Hi [Their Name], Your programming skills are outstanding. As a game developer, I'm eager to connect, discuss coding, and explore potential collaborations. Let's network!"
- "Hello [Their Name], I'm a game programmer passionate about game development. Would be great to connect and exchange ideas with a fellow programmer like you."
- "Hi [Their Name], Your contributions to the gaming industry are impressive. Let's connect as game programmers and discuss the latest trends and technologies."
