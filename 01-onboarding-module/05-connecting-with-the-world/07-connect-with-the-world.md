# **Connect with the world**



## 📝 **Task:**


---

- Search for the Game Developer tag and connect with Game Developers across the world with a meaningful connection note. Use the message prompts provided to you if necessary.
- Simply [Click Here](https://www.linkedin.com/search/results/people/?keywords=game%20developer&origin=FACETED_SEARCH&sid=DY~) and use this filter to connect with game developers across the globe.
- Send a connection request to Outscal Alumni’s telling them how excited you are for taking the first steps in your game dev journey.
- **Note:** You need to log in first to access the page.



## ✅ Submission Guidelines:


---

- If you wish for an engagement boost for your profile or post, feel free to share your LinkedIn profile/post link in build-in-public channel on our discord server.
- Get feedback for improvements from industry professionals for free and be a part of a nurturing community of Game Developers.
- Share your link to the profile in the **#build-in-public** channel of discord.
- Tag your clanmates using your clan roles on discord. For example, if you are in clan-26, use the role `@game-dev-clan-30`  to tag all your clanmates and notify them about your messages in Discord.
- Connect with all members which have shared their profiles in the discord channel to increase your game industry network.
- Submit the link to your LinkedIn profile as a submission for this assignment.
