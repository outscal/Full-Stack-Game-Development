# **LinkedIn Groups**


---



- Game dev LinkedIn groups are like cool hangout spots where you meet fellow devs and might even find some awesome collaborators.
- While you are creating a post on LinkedIn, It is always a good practice to publish that in these groups as well.
- Jumping into discussions and debates helps you level up your critical thinking and problem-solving skills, which are gold in the fast-paced gaming world.
- Who knows, regular chats might even lead to job offers or referrals because recruiters often hang out in these cool communities.
- Join **LinkedIn groups** to expand your network in the Gaming Industry and share your project updates
