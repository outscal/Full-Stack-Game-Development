# **Why should you make connections?**

- You need to understand that when you connect with people on LinkedIn
- - You are adding them to your connection pool
  - You can send them messages after that
  - Start an honest & genuine conversation with them.
- Once you add them to your connections -
- - You get to start seeing what they are sharing on their posts
  - What kind of content they're putting out
  - And they get to engage with your posts as well.
- Most of the jobs that people get in the industry, are actually through connections and references.
- So people often underestimate the power of building the right set of connections as part of your LinkedIn account, which is a slow process.
- But if you invest in it a small amount of time, it can really lead to results where you would never have to worry about applying for jobs.
