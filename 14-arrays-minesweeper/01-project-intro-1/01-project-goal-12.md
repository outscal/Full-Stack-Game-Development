**This is Minesweeper for you!**


Minesweeper is a classic puzzle game that's all about strategy and quick thinking. 

Each click could clear a path to victory or lead to a sudden game-over. That's the thrill of Minesweeper!

You will recreate this classic game with our knowledge of C++ and learn some new things along the way.




> **FUN FACT:** When Microsoft launched their first mouse device, they created Minesweeper for users to get used to the new drag-and-click buttons of the new mouse device.

**The Game Basics:**

- **Winning:** Reveal all the non-mine cells to win.
- **Losing:** Click on a mine, and it's game over, or let the timer run out.
