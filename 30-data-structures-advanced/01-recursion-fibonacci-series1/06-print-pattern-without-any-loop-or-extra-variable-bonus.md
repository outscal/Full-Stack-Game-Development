# **Chapter 1 - Assignment - 3 - Print Pattern Without Any Loop or Extra Variable (Bonus)**

**[Task]** 

- Given a number n, print the pattern described in examples without using any extra variable and any loop.

**[Examples]**

**Input :** 16<br>
**Output :** 16 11 6 1 -4 1 6 11 16 <br>

**Input :** 20<br>
**Output :** 20 15 10 5 0 5 10 15 20 <br>

**Explanation** -  We basically first reduce 5 one by one until we reach a negative or 0. After we reach 0 or negative, we add 5 until we reach n.

**Note:-**

- Use of any loop constructs like while, for..etc is not allowed.
- Solve only using recursion.
