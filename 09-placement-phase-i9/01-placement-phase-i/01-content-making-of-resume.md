**[Key Points While building Resume]**

- Unless you are Bill Gates, your resume **should not be more than 1 page.**
- Put skills on the highest priority on your resume, your skills should be the first thing that should be visible.
- Your resume must be simple with design, do not do anything fancy keep the structure simple and standard.
- **DO NOT USE** any 3rd part software to create a resume. Always use a standard word documented resume so you can use the space per your requirement.
- Add a good technical description of projects rather than putting a lot of co-curricular activities.
- Always put your unique self-made projects on the top and include a Github link for each and every project.
- Your resume must have all critical clickable links on Github, LinkedIn, and portfolio but email should be visible entirely.
