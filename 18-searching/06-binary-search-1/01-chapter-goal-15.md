As you know, Linear search looks for items in a list individually until it finds the target element.

It's great for small, unordered lists but not ideal for massive ones.



Binary Search, on the other hand, can only work with sorted lists.

However, it is a much faster algorithm as it eliminates half of the possibilities with a single search!
