## Game Mechanics


---



- **Turn-Based Combat:** Player and Enemy make their moves one after the other
- **Player abilities:** Player can attack the enemy or heal himself
- **Enemy abilities:** Enemy can attack the Player
- **Restart**: Once the game ends, it can be played again



## Midnight Pizza Fight VS Letho


---



I know you must be thinking, what's the difference between *Midnight Pizza Fight and* the *Letho*? Isn’t it similar, just with a different name?

You are both wrong and right….

Let’s see how



**Difference**

- **Different Story and Different Characters**
- **C# instead of C++**
- **New C# specific concepts:** `var` keyword, Lambda Expressions (`=>`), etc.
- **Better Programming Practices** 



**Similarities**

- **Same Mechanics**



> **💡PRO TIP: *****STORYTELLING***** **
> This game is an example of the power of storytelling. Although it has the same mechanics as Letho, it can provide a completely different experience to the players because it tells a different story and feels different.
