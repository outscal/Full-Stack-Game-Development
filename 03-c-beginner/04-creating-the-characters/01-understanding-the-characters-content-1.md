***Dough Master and Crust Bandit***



## Understanding Player Character


---



Master of doughs, master of pizza-making art, the one and onlyyyyy (drum role🥁 🎶) ***Dough Master***!

*Dough Master* has different mechanics.

- **Health:** The player will have limited health. If it gets less than zero, he dies.
- **Attack:** Don’t underestimate the *Dough Master.* He has a very special weapon, a Dough(which wasn’t obvious at all ☺️), which he uses to slap his enemies around.
- - The attack is called ***Dough Slapper*****!**
- **Heal:** Whenever *Dough Master* feels low on energy, he uses his secret ability to heal himself using a Espresso Shot.
- - This secret ability is called ***Espresso Shot Boost ☕.***



## Understanding Enemy Character


---



To defeat ***Crust Bandit****,* we need to know about his abilities, so let’s look at his abilities.

- **Health:** Just like the player, he also has limited health. If it gets less than zero, he dies.
- **Attack:** He’s not someone to be taken lightly. He is very sneaky and can attack us any time with his ***Sneaky Jab*** 💪.
