In this assignment, you'll be creating three distinct enemy tank types, each with unique characteristics:



**1. Heavy Assault Tank**:

- Attributes: High health, slow speed, and powerful armor-piercing rounds.
- Behavior: Moves slowly, focusing on dealing high damage to the player's tank.

**2. Scout Tank**:

- Attributes: Low health, high speed, and rapid-firing but less damaging bullets.
- Behavior: Agile movements, tries to flank the player and attack from unexpected angles.



![](/Full-Stack-Game-Development/images/0064424e08f7d448.gif)



**3. Artillery Tank**:

- Attributes: Medium health, moderate speed, and long-range high-explosive rounds.
- Behavior: Stays at a distance, aims for area damage, and tries to force the player out of cover.
