- You have these 3 files after making your WebGL Build like discussed in the previous page:
- To upload your game build, first, you will need to compress these files into a `**.zip**` file.
- Next, Click on `**Upload Game Build**` button below this material.
- A Popup will appear in which you will be able to upload your compressed (`**.zip**`) build file and add your Game's name and description.

![Bonus Assignment](//outscal.github.io/Full-Stack-Game-Development/images/fc36e7aaebcec5e8.png)




- Next, you can specify if this build can be played in windows or in Android.
- You will also be able to add the dimensions of you game window. 
- **Please make sure that these dimension are the same one in which you made your game in Unity Editor:**



![sfml](//outscal.github.io/Full-Stack-Game-Development/images/b94df68f1abc819e.png)



- Lastly, select a thumbnail for your game and press the submission button.



![Bonus Assignment](//outscal.github.io/Full-Stack-Game-Development/images/222ca7e49d3d8b0c.png)




- Once you submit your game, you will be redirected to another page where you can play you game and even share this URL with your friends for letting them play your game.
