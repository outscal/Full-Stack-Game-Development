## What is MAT?


---

- It is the opportunity to tell everyone who is the best game developer in this country.
- MAT stands for **Module Assessment Test.** Once you have made some significant progress in your journey you will encounter a MAT.
- **Module Assessment Test** may include:
- - Self-Interview Rounds
  - Paired Interviews
  - Technical Mock Interviews
  - MAT Project (A game project which you will be building from scratch)
- During a MAT you will be assessed on how well you can present and apply the skills you have gained so far.
- Make sure that all the concepts covered in previous modules are crystal clear to you.
- If you have doubts regarding any concepts covered in previous modules, then please interact with your coach and ask your doubts in the discord community.



## Technical Questions in Interviews


---

While answering technical questions in an interview, only knowing the concepts will not help. You will need to make sure that you are following the right approach while answering them.

You can use the basic structure of answering each technical question.

**Structure**

- Explain the concept, in short, using 2 to 3 lines max.
- Always explain the concept with relevant examples in a game.
- Explain the advantages/disadvantages (if any) of the concept.
- End your answer explaining the benefits (in short) of the concept within 2-3 lines max.

## Self-Interview Assignments


---

- There will be multiple Self-Interview assignments in a MAT. In each assignment you will be required to record a video of yourself (Use Loom) answering those questions.
- Each video you record and submit for any self-interview assignment should have a duration of 5 minutes or less.
- More than 5 min. means that you are messing up in answering the question.



## Paired Interview Rounds


---

- Have you ever thought how it feels like to sit on the other side of the table during an interview?
- Paired interviews will give you a new perspective which will help you further in your interviews.
- You will be paired with one of your fellow aspiring developers currently going through the course and you will be interviewing each other by answering some questions given to you.
- You will judging each other as well and giving a status of ‘Passed’ or ‘Failed’



## Mock Interview


---

- You will book an interview slot with a working professional in the gaming industry.
- The mock interview will be of approx. 30 minutes and may be extended further if necessary.
- You will be asked any questions related to game dev and whatever you have learnt so far.
- According to your answers, you will be given a status of ‘Passed’ or ‘Failed’ by the interviewer along with some detailed feedback.
- In case you fail the mock interview, you will need to prepare again and schedule another interview.
