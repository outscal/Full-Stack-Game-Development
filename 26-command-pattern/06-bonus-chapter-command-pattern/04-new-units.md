In this assignment, You must create some new playable characters for the game with what you have learned



![](//outscal.github.io/Full-Stack-Game-Development/images/bddd9d71bc506b9e.gif)



The details of these characters and their executable moves is as follows:



**1. Time Bender**

- Description: A mystical being with control over time, capable of manipulating the flow of battle.
- Actions:
- - **Temporal Shift**: Allows the Time Bender to swap positions with any unit on the battlefield.
  - **Chrono Shield**: Temporarily freezes time for the Time Bender, making it immune to damage for a turn.
  - **Time Ripple**: Sends a wave through the battlefield, reversing the last action taken by each unit (both allies and enemies).



**2. Summoner**

- Description: Masters the summoning of creatures to aid in battles.
- Possible Actions:
- - **Summon Minion**: Calls forth a weak minion to attack or provide support.
  - **Minion Fortress**: loyal minions form a protective barrier around the Summoner, compelling opponents to eliminate these minions before directly attacking the Summoner.
  - **Blood Feast**: Consumes all the health of the summoned minions while sacrificing them and restoring it's own health.
