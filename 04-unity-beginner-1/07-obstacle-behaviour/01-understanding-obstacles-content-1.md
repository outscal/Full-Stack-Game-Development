# Games Without Challenges?


---

Imagine playing a game where your character just walks from start to finish. 

No challenges.

No obstacles.

Just a straight path.



Sounds boring 🥱, right?



Now, think of a game like *Super Mario*. 

What makes it exciting?



**Challenges!**

**Obstacles!**



> **📖 🎮 GAME DEV DICTIONARY**

> ***Obstacle***: "An element in a game that challenges the player, requiring skill, strategy, or quick thinking to overcome. Obstacles add difficulty and engagement to gameplay."



They're the spice that makes games addicting. 

They make you think. 

React faster. 

Improve your skills.



Let's break it down:

1. Challenges add **difficulty💪**
2. They require **strategy🧠**
3. When you overcome them, you feel a sense of **reward💰**





# Challenges in Mr. Blocks


---

Now that you understand why challenges are crucial, let's discuss what you'll build in Mr. Blocks.

But first, put on your game designer hat 🎩



What kind of obstacles do you think would make Mr. Blocks fun and challenging?

Think about obstacles that will test:

- The player's positioning
- Their reaction speed
- Their navigation skills



> 💡**PROTIP**
> When designing obstacles, think about how they complement each other. 
> A mix of different obstacle types can create exciting and challenging level designs!



Got some ideas?🤔 Great! 

Let's see what you'll be building:



![Spikes](https://outscal-assets.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/production/LMS/6675375f717323b2671e88fc/11_06_2024__13_50_24.gif)

**Spikes**



**Rotating Spike** 

   This spike spins continuously. 

   Players will need perfect positioning to get past it.



**Moving Spike**

   This spike moves back and forth. 

   It adds a dynamic challenge to the level.



**Scaling Spike**

   This spike grows and shrinks. 

   Players must watch carefully to pass safely.



Creating various obstacles makes *Mr. Blocks* more engaging and fun. 

Players will need different strategies for each one!
