## What Are Scenes in Unity?


---

Remember how you created your first level for Mr. Blocks? Well, that level was actually something called a "`Scene`" in Unity.

It's like a level that holds all the objects, characters, and environments for a part of the game.

Here's the cool part: your game can have many scenes!

Each level could be its own scene.



## Creating a New Scene


---

Let's create a new scene for your next level:

1. Go to the File menu in Unity.
2. Select New Scene.
3. You'll see a fresh, empty canvas. This is your new level!
4. Save it with a name, like "Level 2".

## Understanding Build Settings


---

Now that you have multiple scenes, you need a way to manage them. That's where Build Settings come in:

1. Go to File > Build Settings.
2. You'll see a panel where you can add and organize your scenes.
3. Click "Add Open Scenes" to add the scene you're currently working on.
