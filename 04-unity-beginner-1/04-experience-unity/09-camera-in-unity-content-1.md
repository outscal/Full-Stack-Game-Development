## What's the Big Deal About Cameras?


---



![Camera In Unity](//outscal.github.io/Full-Stack-Game-Development/images/c675dc3e46511d44.png)



Okay, so what's a camera in Unity? 
It's basically a `GameObject` that shows your player what's happening in the game view. 

Whatever the camera sees, that's what shows up in the Game View.

What will happen if you delete the camera?🤔
