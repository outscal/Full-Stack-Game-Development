# The Scene View


---



When creating any game, you'll place various objects in your level. This includes 2D and 3D assets, UI elements, and more. All of these things will be positioned and arranged in the Scene View.



> 📖 🎮 **UNITY DICTIONARY **
> ***Scene View:*** "A visual workspace where you build and arrange game elements. Every object you place in the Scene View becomes part of your game."



# Understanding the Hierarchy


---



The Hierarchy is your table of contents. Every object in the game, from walls to enemies, is listed here.

When you create something new, it'll show up in the Hierarchy, allowing you to organize and manage everything easily.



> **💡PRO TIP: Organizing Objects in Hierarchy** 
> To organize objects in `Hierarchy`, it’s a good practice to put those similar objects under a common parent object.

# The Inspector


---



The Inspector is your control panel. It's where you can fine-tune each object you've created.

The Inspector allows you to change each object's properties, such as its position, color, or custom behaviors.
