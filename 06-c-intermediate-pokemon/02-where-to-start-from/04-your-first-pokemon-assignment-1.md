**Mission Objective:** You must code the legendary encounter with Professor Oak, where he offers you the chance to choose your first Pokémon!




---



**What You Need to Do:**



       🟢 **Step 1: Greet the Player**

"Introduce yourself to the world of Pokémon! Write an introductory `cout` message from Professor Oak, welcoming the player."



       🟢 **Step 2: Display the Pokémon Choices**

"Professor Oak will present you with three choices of Pokémon: Bulbasaur, Charmander, and Squirtle. You need to display these choices using `cout`."



       🟢 **Step 3: Capture the Player’s Choice**

          "The player will make their first big decision! Take the input using `cin` and store it in a variable called `choice`."




---



**Professor Oak:**



"Well done, Trainer!" 

"But… wait, you think your journey ends with just choosing a Pokémon?" 

"No, no, no!" 

"You need to confirm your choice and see how the game reacts!"



       🟢 **Step 4: Confirm the Choice**

"Write code that reacts to the player’s selection! Use conditional statements (`if-else`) to display which Pokémon the player has chosen and give it a fun description."




---



**Example:**



- If the player chooses **1**, print: `**"You chose Bulbasaur! A wise choice."**`



- If the player chooses **2**, print: `**"You chose Charmander! A fiery choice."**`



- If the player chooses **3**, print: `**"You chose Squirtle! A cool choice."**`



- If the player makes an invalid choice, print: `**"Invalid choice. Please restart the game."**`
