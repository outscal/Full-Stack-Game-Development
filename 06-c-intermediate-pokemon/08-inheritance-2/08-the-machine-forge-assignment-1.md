## **Quest Introduction:**



In the heart of the ancient city of Mechoria, 

lies the Machine Forge, 

a mysterious place where the finest mechanical constructs are brought to life. 



The Forge is overseen by the Forge Masters, 

a guild of engineers, 

who understand the delicate balance between the creation and dismantling of these powerful machines.



Recently, 

a disruption in the Forge’s sequence has caused chaos among the machines, 

threatening to destabilize the entire city. 



As a skilled engineer, 

you are summoned to the Forge to study the order of creation and destruction, 

to restore balance and prevent catastrophic failure.

**Your Mission Objectives:**



- **Step into the Machine Forge:**



- - Your first task is to create the `MasterMachine` class, representing the core machine at the Forge.
  - Define a default constructor and destructor in this class. The constructor should output a message like “Master Machine activated,” and the destructor should signal “Master Machine decommissioned.”



- **Assemble the Assistant Drone:**



- - Create the `AssistantDrone` class, which will inherit from the `MasterMachine`. This drone assists in the Forge’s operations, relying on the core functionalities of the Master Machine.
  - Define the drone’s own constructor and destructor with messages such as “Assistant Drone deployed” for the constructor and “Assistant Drone dismantled” for the destructor.



- **Test the Assembly Line:**



- - In your `main()` function, initiate the creation of a `MasterMachine` object and observe the messages indicating the sequence of activation and shutdown.



- **Deploy the Drone:**



- - Next, create an object of the `AssistantDrone` in the `main()` function to study how the sequence of events unfolds between the core machine and its assistant drone.
