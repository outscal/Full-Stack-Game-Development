Are you excited to develop such a wonderful game? 🥳



But where will you write your code?

Do you have an IDE?

Just a little explanation: IDE is a platform for writing and executing your codes.



If you don't have an IDE, don't worry,

I'll show you the whole process for setting up Visual Studio IDE on your machine.



Step 1 -

Go to any browser and search "visual studio download",

and click on the official website of Microsoft as shown below



![ ](https://outscal-assets.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/production/LMS/634ff2a63bfd3eecf3cfe038/09_21_2024__06_04_53.png)







Step 2 - Click on **Free Download** under **Community** as shown below



![ ](https://outscal-assets.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/production/LMS/634ff2a63bfd3eecf3cfe038/09_21_2024__06_07_25.png)





Step 3 - 

A pop-up window will appear as soon you complete step 2, 

asking you to choose the location to download it. 

Select the **downloads** folder.



![ ](https://outscal-assets.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/production/LMS/634ff2a63bfd3eecf3cfe038/09_21_2024__06_13_58.png)





Step 4 -

An .exe file will be downloaded in your selected location.

Go to that location and double click on .exe file.

A pop up window will appear on the screen



![ ](https://outscal-assets.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/production/LMS/634ff2a63bfd3eecf3cfe038/09_21_2024__06_17_28.png)



![ ](https://outscal-assets.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/production/LMS/634ff2a63bfd3eecf3cfe038/09_21_2024__06_19_00.png)





Step 5 -

Click on continue.

It will start getting downloaded,

and Visual Studio will be opened.



![ ](https://outscal-assets.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/production/LMS/634ff2a63bfd3eecf3cfe038/09_21_2024__06_33_30.png)





Step 6 -

Select the options to install as shown below and click on **install** at right bottom.

It will take some time so have a glass of Water guys!



![ ](https://outscal-assets.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/production/LMS/634ff2a63bfd3eecf3cfe038/09_21_2024__06_38_20.png)

![ ](https://outscal-assets.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/production/LMS/634ff2a63bfd3eecf3cfe038/09_21_2024__06_38_32.png)





Step 7 -

You will see a window shown below, click on **launch**



![ ](https://outscal-assets.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/production/LMS/634ff2a63bfd3eecf3cfe038/09_21_2024__06_45_34.png)





Step 8 -

Now, you can create new folders and open them here.

Then, you can create new files where you will write your code.

The file names and the rest of the information will be provided in further lessons.
