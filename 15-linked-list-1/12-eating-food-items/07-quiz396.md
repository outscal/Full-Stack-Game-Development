- Bounding Box Algorithm: Create a bounding box for each rectangle and compare these boxes for any intersection points.
- Coordinate Comparison Algorithm: Determine if the x-coordinate of the left side of one rectangle is greater than the x-coordinate of the right side of the other and if the y-coordinate of the bottom side of one is above the y-coordinate of the top side of the other.
- Direct Overlap Check: First, check for rectangles with zero area by seeing if the coordinates for any side are equal. Then, determine if one rectangle is completely to the left or right of the other by comparing their x-coordinates, or entirely above or below by comparing y-coordinates.
