## What happens after eating Food?


---

Each food brings a change in the snake

Junk food makes the snake bigger and healthy food shrinks it!



> *The smaller the snake, the lesser the chance to die*



So, The Snake...

**Eats Healthy, Gets Smaller, Lives Longer!**



## How will you change the SNAKE's size?


---



Increasing the snake's size means adding more nodes

And decreasing the size means, deleting the nodes



You have already added nodes at Snake's tail and removed nodes at Snake's head.

Now you will do these insertion and deletion operations at any position!
