# **BONUS ASSIGNMENTS**



As you progress through each module in this course, you'll encounter an opportunity to go beyond and make your game even more exciting through **Bonus Assignments**.



![](//outscal.github.io/Full-Stack-Game-Development/images/2a898cd3d8ffa8c3.png)



## What are Bonus Assignments?


---

- Unlike regular assignments, these tasks are **optional** yet highly recommended
- They provide you with the chance to go the extra mile by implementing new features, mechanics, or aspects in your game projects.
- Unlike other assignments, there won't be provided solutions. It's about you implementing from scratch.
- Your work will be evaluated by reviewers, focusing on code architecture and code quality.
- Feedback will be provided to aid your improvement and understanding, enhancing your coding skills.
- Adding these unique elements can significantly bolster your portfolio or resume.
- Tackling challenges independently will strengthen your problem-solving abilities and expand your coding expertise.
