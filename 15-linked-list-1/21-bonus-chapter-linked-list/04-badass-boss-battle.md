Meet the **Hunter Snake**, an ancient snake that has slept for ages. On a random day, it wakes up, feels incredibly hungry and eats everything it sees. But when it spots the player snake, it gets determined to catch and eat it.



**Your Mission:** Create an Epic Boss Battle in Your Snake Game



#### Key Features of the Hunter Snake & this battle:

- **Mirrored Movement:** The Hunter Snake moves in the exact opposite direction of the Player Snake, creating a unique and challenging dynamic.
- **Food Consumption:** When the Hunter Snake eats a food item, it benefits it similarly to the player.
- **Size:** At the start, the Hunter Snake is double the size of the player snake, making it a powerful opponent.
- **Time Bombs:** Players can deploy Time Bombs that shrink any snake that collides with it.
- **Collision Effects:** Colliding with the Hunter Snake or a Time Bomb will reduce your size to half, making you weak.
- **Avoidance Only:** The Hunter Snake cannot be defeated. Your only option is to avoid it.
