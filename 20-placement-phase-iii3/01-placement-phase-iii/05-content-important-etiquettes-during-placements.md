# **Important Etiquettes during Placements**



[Watch video](https://www.loom.com/embed/01200211f6fe413fae1ceba92842cfae?sid=321fe011-cbfc-4f78-8b78-91a5be9c1ac9)




- **For scheduled interviews, be there on call before 10 minutes and do Pranayama 🧘🏻‍♂️ if you want, this shows your punctuality and seriousness.**
- - For any reason if you can't be there at a decided time, inform the company ASAP, sooner the better and convey your genuine reason **(this reason should not be like I am going to a party/relative’s wedding)**
- When any company reaches out to you but you are not ready to take part in the process yet, you can send a response like below:
  “I am in the process of improving my skill set and I believe that I would be able to do justice to this opportunity in another ‘X’ months since by then I would have the skill sets and the experience to do so. Would love to stay in touch with you for future opportunities. Thanks & Regards Your Name“
  This is just an example, you can and should reply in a way that is authentic to you.
- When any company reaches out to you...
- 1. Reply ASAP with some generic gratitude message and say thanks.
  2. - every company runs low on time, the more time you are wasting, the lesser your chances to get selected.
  3. Inform the Outscal team immediately by email - [clan-operations@outscal.com](mailto:clan-operations@outscal.com)
  4. - It is a very important step to keep the team updated about your progress.
  5. **Make sure to update Outscal team regarding the result of every step of the placement process.** This is very important to crack the best offers out there in the market.
- **When a company has given you an assignment/test but you are not able to submit it before the deadline...**
- - As soon as you understand you are gonna be late for the submission, even if it's 1 min late or 1 day late, it's your responsibility to convey that **ASAP**.
- **If you have any doubts related to anything, that might be assignment/test, the offer company has sent, the terms and conditions...**
- - Reply immediately and clarify all your doubts from the company directly.
  - Be polite and professional while asking any doubts or raising any concerns.
- **When a company has given you an assignment/test but you are not able to complete it fully but still developed a partial solution**
- - Before submitting a partial assignment, first, reach out to Outscal and share the problems that you are facing.
  - It's ok if you have tried your 100% and you could not crack the entire problem statement. First, understand, can you solve the entire assignment by asking for some buffer time or not
  - If you can solve the assignment with some extra time, go ahead and ask for it
  - If you cant solve the assignment even after getting extra time and even after talking with Outscal, then it's fine to submit it partially, but first, confirm this with Outscal.
