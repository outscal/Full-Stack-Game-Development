<br>
It's important not to bombard gaming industry recruiters with job requests or inquiries when you're still a stranger. Here's why:
<ul>
<li>Sending lots of messages can make you seem unprofessional and might rub them the wrong way.</li>
<li>Hitting up someone you don't know with job requests can be awkward for both parties. It's better to get to know each other a bit first.</li>
<li>Networking is all about building relationships over time. So, take it easy and focus on nurturing relationships with recruiters and industry professionals.</li>
<li>This approach can eventually lead to more meaningful job opportunities down the road.</li>
<li>So, before diving into job talk, take the time to connect and build a rapport. It's a friendlier and more effective way to go about networking.</li>
</ul>
