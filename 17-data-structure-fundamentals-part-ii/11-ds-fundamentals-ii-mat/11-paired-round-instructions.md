## **Instructions**


---

- To request for a Paired Round Interview, please select your current module in this form and submit it:   [*form_link*](https://airtable.com/appEW3Ugj0ETmaLsi/shr7ERnFs33PjTAPO)
- Once you submit the form, someone will get paired with you and depending upon your and interviewer’s availability , the interview will be conducted.
- Once you get paired with someone, both of you will receive a unique set of question over email along with contact details of your paired partner for this round.
- You must coordinate and connect with each other for the conducting the paired round interview according to your availability.
- The Paired Round will follow the following structure:



## Structure of Paired Round Interviews:


---

- **[Round 1] → 30 mins**
- 1. **Person 1 (P1) → Interviewer** and **Person 2 (P2) → Interviewee**
  2. 1. Time: 20 mins
    2. Person 1 will ask all the P1 questions.
    3. Person 2 will answer all the P1 Questions.
  3. **Feedback discussion** (verbal, give detailed feedback to P2)
  4. 1. Time: 10 mins.
    2. Share how he/she can improve.
- **[Round 2]**
- 1. **Person 2 (P2) → Interviewer** and **Person 1 (P1) → Interviewee**
  2. 1. Time: 20 mins
    2. Person 2 will ask all the P2 questions.
    3. Person 1 will answer all the P2 Questions.
  3. **Feedback discussion** (verbal, give detailed feedback to P1)
  4. 1. Time: 10 mins.
    2. Share how he/she can improve.



## Submission Guidelines:


---

- Once the paired round interview is done you will recieve a feedback form to fill for the other person in an email.
- You must give honest unfiltered feedback to your partner so they can improve their shortcomings.
- You will be judged based on the feedback you give to the other person.
